**Anti-patterns:**
- Back-to-back meetings all day
- No deep work blocks
- Skipping 1:1s when "busy"

## Executive Team Meetings

**Weekly staff meeting (60-90 min):**
1. Metrics review (15 min)
2. Key decisions needed (30 min)
3. Cross-functional blockers (15 min)
4. Announcements (10 min)

**Quarterly offsite (1-2 days):**
- Strategy review
- Team dynamics
- Planning for next quarter
- No laptops, real conversation

## Hiring Executives

**Process:**
1. Define role and scorecard (outcomes, not activities)
2. Source: referrals, executive recruiters, personal network
3. Initial screen (CEO or COO)
4. Reference calls BEFORE final rounds
5. Team interviews
6. Offer and close

**Red flags:**
- Can't articulate past failures
- Blames others for misses
- Overvalues their contribution vs team
- Doesn't ask hard questions about the company

## Firing Executives

**When to act:**
- Pattern of misses (not one bad quarter)
- Culture misalignment (values, not style)
- Team feedback is consistently negative
- You're managing around them, not through them

**How to act:**
- Decide and act quickly once clear
- Separation terms: be generous, be fast
- Communication: brief, respectful, forward-looking
- Don't let them linger

## CEO Self-Management

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Burnout | Protect sleep, exercise, vacation |
| Isolation | External confidants, CEO peer groups |
| Decision fatigue | Reduce trivial decisions, delegate |
| Echo chamber | Seek disagreement, front-line exposure |
| Imposter syndrome | Normal; the job IS hard |

**Remember:** The team watches your mood. Anxiety spreads. Calm spreads.
