# Visitor Attractions - Seattle

## Must-See (Actually Worth It)

### Pike Place Market
- **Go early**: 9am before tourist crush
- **Skip**: Generic souvenir shops
- **Do**: Watch fish throwing, original Starbucks (just for photo), Beecher's cheese, Pike Place Chowder
- **Pro tip**: Explore lower levels (Post Alley, gum wall)

### Space Needle
- **Verdict**: Worth it once, especially at sunset
- **Cost**: ~$35-40
- **Alternative**: Columbia Center Sky View ($25, higher, less crowded)
- **Best time**: Clear day, golden hour

### Chihuly Garden and Glass
- **Verdict**: Genuinely stunning, even for non-art people
- **Duration**: 1-2 hours
- **Combo**: Get Space Needle combo ticket

### Museum of Pop Culture (MoPOP)
- **Verdict**: Excellent for music/sci-fi fans
- **Highlights**: Nirvana, Hendrix, sci-fi/fantasy exhibits
- **Building**: Frank Gehry design worth seeing alone

### Olympic Sculpture Park
- **Verdict**: Free, beautiful, underrated
- **Best for**: Sunset, mountain views on clear days

### Seattle Waterfront
- **Reality check**: Under major construction through 2026
- **Still good**: Ferries, Seattle Aquarium, Ye Olde Curiosity Shop

| Attraction | Why Go | Skip If |
|------------|--------|---------|
| Ballard Locks | Watch salmon, boats, free | Raining hard |
| Discovery Park | Best urban park, lighthouse | Short on time |
| Capitol Hill | Walk Broadway, coffee, nightlife | Not into urban |
| Fremont Troll | Quick photo op | Expecting more |

| University of Washington | Cherry blossoms (March), beautiful campus | Outside spring |

## Tourist Traps (Skip or Minimize)

### Gum Wall
- **Reality**: 5-minute photo op max
- **Gross factor**: Yes

### Original Starbucks
- **Reality**: Same coffee as any Starbucks, long line
- **Do**: Quick photo of the sign, move on

### Seattle Underground Tour
- **Mixed reviews**: Some love it, some find it slow
- **Alternative**: Bill Speidel version is best if you go

### Duck Tours
- **Skip**: Overpriced, cheesy
- **Better**: Take a ferry (cheaper, better views)

## Free Activities

1. **Pike Place Market** - Walking and watching is free
2. **Olympic Sculpture Park** - Always free
3. **Discovery Park** - Trails and lighthouse
4. **Gas Works Park** - Skyline views
5. **Capitol Hill walking** - Architecture, shops, coffee
6. **First Thursday Art Walk** - Pioneer Square galleries
7. **Ballard Locks** - Engineering marvel, salmon ladder
8. **U-District farmers market** - Saturday mornings

## Seasonal Considerations

| Season | Best Attractions | Avoid |
|--------|------------------|-------|
| Summer (Jul-Sep) | Outdoor parks, ferries, day trips | Indoor museums (waste of nice weather) |
| Fall (Oct-Nov) | Museums, coffee culture, food | Mountain hikes (weather unpredictable) |
| Winter (Dec-Feb) | Holiday markets, museums, food | Outdoor attractions (grey, wet) |
| Spring (Mar-Jun) | UW cherry blossoms, tulips | Crowds at Pike Place |
